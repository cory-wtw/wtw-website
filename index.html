The Mission

They earned it.
We help them access it.

VA disability benefits aren't charity. They're compensation — earned through service and sacrifice. Most unsheltered veterans don't know what they're owed, and the VA process can easily frustrate anyone who tries to navigate it alone.

Worth Their Weight embeds in the Chattanooga community to find veterans where they are. When we find one, we connect them with a VSO partner who handles the VA claims process — because no veteran should have to navigate it alone.

"Chattanooga ended veteran homelessness once in 2019. The numbers crept back up. Worth Their Weight exists to finish what this community started — and this time make it permanent."

The Problem

Most unsheltered veterans have earned VA disability benefits they've never claimed. No one showed them how.

The Solution

Street outreach finds veterans. VSO referral connects them to monthly tax-free income and access to VA healthcare, which may include long-term coverage depending on eligibility and disability rating.

The Goal

Functional zero veteran homelessness in Hamilton County — 15 to 20 veterans connected to benefits in year one.

The Stakes

This is what a single claim could be worth.

$180–$3,939 Monthly, tax-free

VA disability compensation ranges from 10% to 100% rating. We help ensure veterans are connected with the people who can evaluate and pursue all eligible benefits.

$1M+ Lifetime value

In some cases, particularly for younger veterans with high disability ratings, lifetime tax-free income can exceed one million dollars — plus access to VA healthcare.

Access VA Healthcare

A successful claim can unlock access to VA healthcare, which may include long-term coverage depending on eligibility and disability rating — including mental health care, addiction treatment, and primary care.

How We Work

Two programs.
One mission.

We find veterans in the community and connect them with the accredited representatives who can help.

Program 01

Veteran Recon

Veteran Recon is our street outreach program. Field Scouts embed in the community — visiting shelters, attending community events, and building relationships with local organizations to identify veterans. No clipboard. No intake form. No clinical lens. Just genuine human contact and the primary mission: find veterans and build the trust that makes everything else possible.

Program 02

Benefits Support & Referral

When our Field Scouts identify a veteran, we connect them with a VSO partner who handles the VA claims process. Worth Their Weight's role is identification and connection — we find the veterans, then ensure they reach the accredited representatives who can pursue their claims.

Worth Their Weight is not a law firm and does not provide legal representation before the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs. All claims-related services are performed by VA-accredited attorneys, agents, or Veterans Service Organizations.

Our Story

Built from a backpack
and a walk downtown.

In 2021, Cory Gold lived on Riverfront Parkway. Every day he walked downtown with a backpack full of supplies — handing them out to unsheltered neighbors because it was the right thing to do. No org. No funding. No plan. Just a person who decided to show up.

Worth Their Weight is the infrastructure Cory built around that original instinct. As a disabled veteran, Cory navigated the same system these veterans face. He knows how hard it is — and he decided to make sure no veteran has to do it alone.
